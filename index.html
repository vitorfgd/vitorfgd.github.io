Radar Warfare

April 2016 – November 2017

After developing some mobile games I decided it was time to take a step further on game development, I planned, developed (both code and arts) and launched Radar Warfare over the course of a year and a half.

Radar Warfare is a bullet hell / fast-paced shoot 'em up with radar theme and retro style. In the game, you will face an alien invasion. As a commander, it's your duty to fight back and save the world.

Available on Steam.
