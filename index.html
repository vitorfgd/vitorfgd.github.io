Radar Warfare

April 2016 – November 2017

After developing some mobile games I decided it was time to take a step further on game development. I planned, developed (both code and arts) and launched Radar Warfare over the course of a year and a half.

Radar Warfare is a bullet hell / fast-paced shoot 'em up with radar theme and retro style. In the game, you will face an alien invasion. As a commander, it's your duty to fight back and save the world.

C# - Unity3D - Photoshop - Windows - Linux - Mac - Steam

REVI

2016 – 2016

REVI is a news reader app. It was developed after a partnership between two universities (Instituto Federal Catarinense and Bom Jesus IELUSC) to improve both university journals.

I worked as full stack developer. REVI was created using Ionic and Angular with a PHP API on server side in the course of two months.

Ionic - Angular - PHP - Photoshop - Android - iOS

HARD

2012 – 2014

HARD was my first published game/application. My role has primarily been lead artist on the first public version. I was assigned as fullstack developer using Unity3D and C# on later versions of the game.

The game was primarily developed using Construct 2 and exported as HTML and Javascript. Later versions were ported to Unity and exported as native applications.

HARD has been installed over 100.000 times.
